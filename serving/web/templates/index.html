In plain language

Fund health — active share

How much of each fund actually differs from its benchmark. Low active share paired with an active-fund fee is what's sometimes called closet indexing — paying active-management fees for index-like exposure. This is a description of what the numbers show, not a recommendation.

Style drift

Cap-size composition of a scheme, month by month, against AMFI's official large/mid/small classification. A flexi-cap fund creeping toward 95% large-cap is drift worth knowing about.

Inferred turnover

Approximated portfolio churn from month-over-month holdings changes. A lower bound only — intra-month round trips and price drift both hide from monthly disclosure.

Industry crowding & liquidity

Sessions the mutual fund industry would need to exit a stock at 20% of average daily volume. A relative ranking of crowding, not a forecast of exit time.

Fee drag calculator

Terminal-value difference between two expense ratios, under a stated return assumption. An illustration, never a prediction — change the assumption and watch the gap move.
